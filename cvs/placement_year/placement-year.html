Placement Year

Support Astronomer Placement Year

Isaac Newton Group (ING), 2022 - 2023

La Palma

Overview

The ING placement year involves working as a support astronomer on the 2.5m Isaac Newton Telescope. The telescope has two instruments: the Wide Field Camera (WFC) and the Intermediate Dispersion Spectrometer (IDS). Job roles include creating observing plans and setting up the instruments according to the specifications within them. Also encoraged is working on projects and submitting our own proposals to the telescope.

See the instrument setup notes here!

Projects:

skyWATCH

Developing the skyWATCH all-sky camera and weather monitor
EURONEAR

Observing and detecting NEAs over two observing nights and determining their properties
