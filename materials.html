    Astronomy

    Astronomy is the study of the stars, planets, the Universe, and everything within it. Simultaneously, it is both the oldest and newest science. For thousands of years, humankind has looked to the stars and pondered, charted, monitored, and gazed in awe. More recently, with the advent of modern technology, time itself has become something we can look through as we look ever deeper into the great expanse.

    Astronomy is unlike other sciences, as physical experimentation is mostly impossible. Instead, observational and theoretical studies dominate the field - where astronomers have developed ingenious ways of studying that which we may never touch.
